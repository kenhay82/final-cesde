What we DO?

Early stimulation allows the animal to be put in completely new situations in a controlled environment, which allows free interaction of the individual with the universe that surrounds it.

Stimulation can occur at different levels:
  • Physical.
  • Mental.
  • Emotional.

take a walk as a tool to work with the animal regardless of the individual approach. The walk, with its number of variations, can be used in cases of:

  • Physical therapy.
  • Physical development.
  • Development of emotional capacities.
  • Stress regulator.
  • Cognitive development.

Teaching different forms of communication allows the animal to understand and feel safe in a greater number of spaces, generating better self-regulation and improving stress levels.

It is very important to keep in mind that the animal interprets all the communicative forms in the environment, which is why the work of all those involved is necessary.
